### 👋 About Me

I'm a **Software Engineer** at **[ZarPin](https://www.linkedin.com/company/zarpin)**, working on a gold-focused fintech platform across **Android** and **web**. I care about clean architecture, end-to-end ownership, and shipping features that feel great to use.

Before ZarPin, I built blockchain and fintech products at **NextBlocks (Bitpin)**, led frontend for an AI-powered platform at **GOODFOLIO**, and contributed to financial platforms at **Charisma Financial Group** and **Rahnema College**.

My path started in biomedical engineering — research on contrast media injector UIs, convex optimization, and Android — and that curiosity still drives how I approach software today.

When I'm not coding, you'll find me exploring **music**, learning **French**, or writing about **web performance**, JavaScript, and how the internet actually works.
